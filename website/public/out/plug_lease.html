plug_lease

A plug to an external service to maintain leases on CAs.

A lease protects a binding between a CA id and a location. This binding can only be changed by the owner of the lease, avoiding duplication of CAs across the data center.

If the node owning the lease crashes, the lease eventually expires. At that point a different, randomly picked node can grab a new lease and recreate the CA safely.
